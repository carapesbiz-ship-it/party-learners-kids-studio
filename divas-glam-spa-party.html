Frequently Asked Questions

Are boys welcome?

Boys are welcome too. Décor, robes and theme colors can be adapted for mixed groups with color-friendly options like blue, green, gold, purple or any custom palette.

What ages is this best for?

Designed for ages 5–12. Activities can also be adapted to teens and adult celebrations by request. We'll help tailor the setup to your group.

Can we choose colors or themes?

Yes! Every party can be personalized around your child's favorite colors or a theme — Barbie-inspired, Frozen-inspired, Disney Princess, Pink Bow, Angel & Stitch, K-pop / Demon Hunters-inspired and more. Themed styling examples are inspired only and not affiliated with any character or brand owners.

Are the masks applied by the children?

Yes — the colorful spa-style masks are applied by the children themselves with support and guidance from the host. We use gentle, kid-friendly spa-style products.

Are real cucumbers used?

No — the spa-style cucumber relaxation moment uses simulated cucumber pieces, so it's mess-free and comfortable for every child.

How long is the party?

Approx. 2 hours of hosted activities — spa time, glam, runway, games, photos and certificates. Super Luxury VIP SPA can run longer.

What's the difference between the Divas packages?

Glam Diva SPA Time is the core spa & glam experience. Full Glam Diva Experience includes everything from Glam Diva SPA Time plus favorite-theme styling inspired by your child's chosen theme. Super Luxury VIP SPA is the ultimate photo-ready tier — adds full themed décor, individual teepees decorated with lights (when space allows), personalized robe details, a decorated VIP luxury chair, jewelry station with bracelet-making, balloon garland and a printed group photo for each girl.

Is the Glitter Bar included?

The Glitter Bar is an optional add-on, not automatically included in a package. It's a photo-ready sparkle station you can add to any Divas Glam tier or Mommy & Me Spa Day — just ask and we'll let you know what's available.

What's the difference between Super Luxury VIP SPA and Slumber Party Teepee Setup?

Super Luxury VIP SPA is a spa/glam party package — its teepees are part of the spa setup with premium styling. Slumber Party Teepee Setup is a separate overnight sleepover setup with bedding, themed décor, soft lighting, setup and next-day pickup. Slumber does not automatically include spa activities, robes, balloon garlands, jewelry station, VIP chair or the Glitter Bar — those can be requested separately when available.

Are you licensed?

Yes — Party Learners Kids Studio is fully licensed, permitted, and insured for hosted party experiences, for peace of mind.
