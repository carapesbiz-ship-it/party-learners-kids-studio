Frequently Asked Questions

Are boys welcome?

Boys are welcome too. Décor, robes and theme colors can be adapted for mixed groups with color-friendly options like blue, green, gold, purple or any custom palette.

Are the spa products safe for kids?

Yes — we use gentle, kid-friendly spa-style products and guided activities. The colorful masks are applied by the children themselves with support from the host, and the cucumber relaxation moment uses spa-style simulated cucumber slices. Everything is designed to feel fun, age-appropriate and comfortable.

What about hairstyling?

Hairstyling includes colorful extensions for a fun glam look. The extensions are gentle, removable and can be kept when included in the package.

What ages is this best for?

Designed for ages 5–12. Activities can also be adapted to teens and adult celebrations by request. We'll help tailor the setup to your group.

How long is the party?

Approx. 2 hours of hosted activities — spa time, glam, runway, games, photos and certificates. Super Luxury VIP SPA can run longer.

What's the difference between the packages?

Glam Diva SPA Time is the core spa & glam experience. Full Glam Diva Experience includes everything from Glam Diva SPA Time plus favorite-theme styling inspired by your child's chosen theme. Super Luxury VIP SPA is the ultimate photo-ready spa celebration — adds full themed décor, individual teepees decorated with lights (when space allows), personalized robe details, a decorated VIP luxury chair for the birthday girl, jewelry station with bracelet-making, balloon garland and a printed group photo for each girl.

How many kids can join?

Most setups work beautifully for 6–20 guests. Larger groups may be available by request depending on the location, space and selected package.

Are you licensed?

Yes — Party Learners Kids Studio is fully licensed, permitted, and insured for hosted party experiences, for peace of mind.
