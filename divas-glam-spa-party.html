Important Details

Group & Setup

  • Minimum 6 children per package
  • Setup starts 60 minutes before the party
  • Required space and access to water
  • 1–2 adults required to assist during spa time
  • Boys are welcome too — great for mixed groups and siblings

Travel & Location

  • Travel fee not included; depends on location
  • Outdoor summer parties available at parks/playgrounds
  • Service area: Abbotsford & Lower Mainland, BC

Safety & Booking

  • Guided self-application only
  • Limited weekend spots — book early
  • 50% deposit required to secure your date
Pricing depends on theme, location, number of guests and selected experience. Contact us for pricing and availability.
