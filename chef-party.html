Frequently Asked Questions

Do you cook or prepare the food?

No. This is a creative decorating-only experience. Families provide all food items and ingredients, such as cupcakes, mini pizza bases, fruit, vegetables, frosting, sauces and toppings. Party Learners Kids Studio does not cook, bake, prepare, cut, serve or handle food preparation — we provide the themed setup, decorating tools, activity guidance, and a clear preparation list before the party.

Who provides the food?

Parents provide all food items and ingredients. If preferred, families can ask about pre-purchased, store-bought packaged materials when available. Party Learners Kids Studio does not cook, bake, prepare or serve food.

What tools do the kids use?

Kids use only kid-friendly decorating tools — plastic spreaders, decorating spatulas, and themed accessories. There are no sharp tools, no heat sources, and no real cooking equipment.

What activities can we choose?

Pick any two: Mini Pizza Decorating, Cupcake Decorating, Fruit Character Creations, or Vegetable Fun Art. All food items are parent-provided, and kids decorate them using kid-friendly decorating tools.

What ages is this best for?

Recommended for ages 6 and up. Activities are guided and adapted so younger kids and tweens can both participate comfortably.

What if we are short on time?

Ask us about the option to have packaged, store-bought materials purchased in advance when available. Items remain unopened/sealed whenever applicable and are used for decorating only.

Can we customize the theme?

Yes — the Chef Party can be styled around your child's favorite character, color story or trend, available by request. Themed styling is inspired only and not affiliated with any character or brand owners.

Are allergies handled by Party Learners?

Parents are responsible for choosing allergy-safe ingredients and supervising any food-specific needs, since all food is parent-provided. Let us know in advance and we'll plan the activity around it.

How long is the party?

Approx. 2 hours of hosted activities — decorating, music, games, mini creation showcase and certificates.

Are you licensed?

Yes — Party Learners Kids Studio is fully licensed, permitted, and insured for hosted party experiences, for peace of mind.
