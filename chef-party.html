Frequently Asked Questions

Do you cook or prepare the food?

No. This is a creative decorating-only experience. Parents provide all food items and ingredients, such as cupcakes, mini pizza bases, fruit, vegetables, frosting, sauces and toppings. Party Learners Kids Studio provides the themed setup, decorating tools, activity guidance, and a clear preparation list before the party.

Who provides the food?

Parents provide all food items and ingredients. If preferred, families can ask about pre-purchased, store-bought packaged materials when available. Party Learners Kids Studio does not cook, bake, prepare or serve food.

Short on time? Can you bring packaged materials?

Ask us about the option to have packaged, store-bought materials purchased in advance when available. Items remain unopened/sealed whenever applicable and are used for decorating only.

What about food allergies?

Parents are responsible for choosing allergy-safe ingredients and supervising any food-specific needs. Let us know in advance and we'll plan around it.

What tools do the kids use?

Kids use only kid-friendly decorating tools — plastic spreaders, decorating spatulas, and themed accessories. There are no sharp tools, no heat sources, and no real cooking equipment.

How long is the party?

Approx. 2 hours of hosted activities — decorating, music, games, mini creation showcase and certificates.

Do parents need to clean up?

We bring table protection and cleaning supplies for the activity area. Parents handle the food itself.

What ages is this best for?

Designed for ages 6–12. Activities are guided and adapted so younger kids and tweens can both participate comfortably.

How many kids can join?

Most Chef Party setups work for 6–14 children. Larger groups are available by request depending on your space and selected activities.

Are you licensed?

Yes — Party Learners Kids Studio is fully licensed, permitted, and insured for hosted party experiences, for peace of mind.
