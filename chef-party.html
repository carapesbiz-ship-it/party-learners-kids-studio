Chef Party
Experience

A hands-on decorating party where kids feel like real little chefs. Choose two creative food-decorating activities and enjoy a fully hosted setup with music, games, chef stations and a mini creation showcase.

Request Package Details View Activities
This is a creative decorating experience only. Party Learners Kids Studio does not cook, bake or prepare food. Parents provide all food items and ingredients, or may request pre-purchased / store-bought materials when available. We provide the setup, decorating materials, activity guidance and a preparation list.
step one ✿

Choose 2 Activities to Decorate

Pick the two creative food-decorating activities your little chefs will love most.

Mini Pizza Decorating

Little chefs add toppings, sauces and creative shapes to their personal pizza canvas.

Cupcake Decorating

Frosting, sprinkles, sparkles and toppers — each child decorates their own cupcake set.

Fruit Character Creations

Turn fresh fruit into smiling characters and edible art with our guided fruit shapes.

Vegetable Fun Art

Playful vegetable arrangements where every plate becomes a little masterpiece.
