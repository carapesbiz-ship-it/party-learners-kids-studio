Everything You Need for a Beautiful Paint Party

From the boho-styled table to individual art kits and a hosted creative experience — we bring it all to you.

Boho Table Setup

  • Boho-style decorated table
  • Backdrop & table decoration
  • 1 boho table for up to 8 kids
  • 2 tables for 8+ kids

Art Materials Per Child

  • Individual medium-size canvas
  • Craft crowns or mirrors to decorate
  • Paint brushes & child-safe acrylics
  • Apron, water cup & paint palette
  • Table protection & cleaning supplies
  • Decoration materials

Host / Cosplay Artist

  • Professional, warm host
  • Encouragement & creative support

Activities Included

  • Guided painting experience
  • Art & movement-based games

  • Short interactive breaks
  • Free-paint time for final touches

  • Art gallery-style display of finished paintings
  • Each child is presented as an artist
  • Every child takes their artwork home
  • Participation certificate
