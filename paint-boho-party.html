Frequently Asked Questions

Are the paints kid-friendly?

Yes — we use kid-friendly washable acrylic paints and guided activities designed for an age-appropriate creative experience. We also provide aprons and table protection so clothing and surfaces are protected.

Does each child take their canvas home?

Yes — every child takes home both their painted canvas and their decorated craft (crown or mirror).

How long is the party?

Approx. 1.5 hours of hosted activities — guided painting, craft decorating, music, games, mini art exhibition and certificates.

Can the theme be customized?

Yes. We can style the boho setup around your child's favorite theme — Barbie-inspired, Frozen-inspired, Capybara, Pink Bow and more. Ask us about custom ideas.

How many kids can join?

Best experience is 6–14 children. For larger groups, ask us — we'll let you know what's possible.

Do you travel outside Abbotsford?

Yes — we serve the Lower Mainland, BC. Transportation isn't included and may vary depending on distance and location.
