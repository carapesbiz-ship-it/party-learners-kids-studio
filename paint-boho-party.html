good to know ✿

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages is this best for?

Designed for ages 5–12. Guided painting is adapted so younger kids and tweens can both feel proud of what they create.

What do kids take home?

Every child takes home both their painted canvas and their decorated craft (crown or mirror), plus a participation certificate.

Can we choose a theme?

Yes. We can style the boho setup around your child's favorite theme — Barbie-inspired, Frozen-inspired, Capybara, Pink Bow and more, or a custom color palette. Themes labeled "inspired" are not affiliated with any character or brand owners.

Are materials kid-friendly?

Yes — we use kid-friendly washable acrylic paints and guided, age-appropriate activities. We also provide aprons and table protection so clothing and surfaces are protected.

How long is the experience?

Approx. 1.5 hours of hosted activities — guided painting, craft decorating, music, games, mini art exhibition and certificates.

Is the Glitter Bar included?

The Glitter Bar is an optional add-on, not included by default. It's a photo-ready sparkle station you can add to your Paint Boho Party — just ask and we'll let you know what's available.

Can this be customized?

Absolutely — themes, colors, décor add-ons and group size can all be tailored. Tell us your ideas and we'll help build the right setup.

How many kids can join?

Best experience is 6–14 children. For larger groups, ask us — we'll let you know what's possible.

Do you travel outside Abbotsford?

Yes — we serve the Lower Mainland, BC. Transportation isn't included and may vary depending on distance and location.
