Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the same as Super Luxury VIP SPA?

No. Slumber Party Teepee Setup is a separate overnight sleepover setup. Super Luxury VIP SPA is a spa/glam party package with premium spa styling. Slumber does not automatically include spa activities, robes, garlands or VIP spa extras.

Does Slumber include spa activities?

No — spa activities are not automatically included. They can be requested as an optional add-on when available.

Are robes included?

Robes are not automatically included in the base Slumber setup. They can be requested as an optional add-on when available.

Is a balloon garland included?

A balloon garland is not automatically included. It can be added as an optional premium décor add-on when available.

When do you pick everything up?

We set up the teepees, bedding, décor and lights, and return the next day for pickup. Setup and next-day pickup are included.

How much space do we need?

Each guest needs room for an individual teepee with a mattress. Tell us your guest count and room size, and we'll let you know the best setup for your space.

Can we choose a theme?

Yes — the teepee styling can be themed around your child's favorite colors or characters. Themed styling is inspired only and not affiliated with any character or brand owners.

Can we add the Glitter Bar or spa extras?

Yes — the Glitter Bar, spa activities, robes, balloon garlands, jewelry station and VIP chair styling can all be requested separately as optional add-ons when available.
