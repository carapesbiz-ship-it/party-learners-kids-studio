Vancouver Kids Spa Party FAQ

Do you travel to Vancouver for kids spa parties?

Yes — we serve Vancouver and the surrounding Lower Mainland, including Burnaby, Surrey, Richmond, Coquitlam and North Vancouver. We bring the full setup to your home or venue. Transportation is not included and may vary depending on distance from Abbotsford.

What's included in a Vancouver kids spa party?

Colorful robes during the party, spa-style masks the children apply themselves with host guidance, cucumber relaxation moment, mini makeovers, hairstyling with colorful extensions, glitter tattoos, music, games and a runway moment. We provide setup, materials, decoration and a professional host.

How many guests can join?

Most setups work beautifully for 6 to 20 guests. Larger groups may be available by request depending on the location, space and selected package.

What ages is this best for?

Designed for ages 5–12. Activities can also be adapted to teens and adult celebrations by request. We'll help tailor the setup to your group.

Are you licensed and permitted?

Yes — Party Learners Kids Studio is fully licensed, permitted, and insured for hosted party experiences, for peace of mind.

How do I book a date for Vancouver?

Tell us your preferred date, location, party experience and number of guests using the Request Details form. We'll reply within 24 hours with availability and package details. Weekend dates fill quickly — let's chat early.
