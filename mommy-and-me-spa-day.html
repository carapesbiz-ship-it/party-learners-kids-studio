Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mommy & Me Spa Day a kids party package?

No. Mommy & Me Spa Day is a standalone specialty experience designed for bonding between a mom and her little one — slower, sweeter and spa-inspired. It is not a children's party package and not a simple add-on.

What's included?

A styled spa setup, gentle spa-inspired activities, quality time together, photo-ready styling and sweet bonding moments. Robes for Mommy & Me and a natural skincare workshop are included with select setups. Details may vary by selected setup and add-ons.

Are robes included?

Matching spa robes for Mommy & Me are included with select setups. Tell us what you're looking for and we'll confirm what's included for your experience.

Can we add the Glitter Bar or extra styling?

Yes — optional add-ons such as the Glitter Bar, balloon garland, premium décor, personalized details and gift bags can be requested when available.

Is it available in Spanish?

Yes — Spanish-speaking experiences are available. Fiestas disponibles en español.

Can we choose the styling?

Yes — the spa setup can be styled around your preferred colors and aesthetic for a calm, photo-ready experience.
