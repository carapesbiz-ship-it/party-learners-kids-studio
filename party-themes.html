Compare Our 3 Active Experiences

Each experience is designed end-to-end — décor, materials, host, music and activities — so parents can simply enjoy the celebration.

Signature

Divas Glam Spa Party

  • Best for: Birthdays who want a sparkling, pampering celebration.
  • Main activities: Colorful robes, playful facial masks, cucumber relaxation, mini makeovers, hairstyling with colorful extensions, runway, dance & games.
  • Great for: Boys & girls, ages 5–12, 6–14 guests.
  • Duration: Approx. 2 hours, fully hosted.

Paint Boho Party + Craft

  • Best for: Creative kids who love art and want to take something handmade home.
  • Main activities: Guided canvas painting, craft decorating (crown or mirror), free-paint, art-themed games, glitter tattoos, mini art exhibition.
  • Great for: Boys & girls, ages 5–12, 6–14 guests.
  • Duration: Approx. 1.5 hours, fully hosted.

Chef Party – Decorating Experience

  • Best for: Kids who love hands-on, food-themed creativity (decorating only — we don't cook or bake).
  • Main activities: Choose 2 — Mini Pizza Decorating, Cupcake Decorating, Fruit Character Creations, Vegetable Fun Art. Plus glitter tattoos, music, games, mini creation showcase.
  • Great for: Boys & girls, ages 5–12, 6–14 guests.
  • Duration: Approx. 2 hours, fully hosted.
